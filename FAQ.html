Frequently Asked Questions

How does the transfer work?

The user enters the amount that they want to request into the corresponding textbox. The person receiving the request will be notified about the request. From there, they will be able to approve or deny the request. If approved, the money will be sent from the sender's account to the requester.

Is it secure?

Yes! The process is encrypted, so your money and information are completely protected.
